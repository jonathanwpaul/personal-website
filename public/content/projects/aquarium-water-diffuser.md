When doing water changes, pouring water back into the tank stirs up the substrate and disturbs fish. A diffuser spreads the flow so you can refill without making a mess of the tank.

This is a remix of [Zakoplex's original design](https://www.printables.com/model/761514), redrawn from scratch in Fusion 360 with several improvements:

- Perforated bottom with a removable cover for increased flow control
- Separate mounting bracket that clips to the tank rim, attached with two M4 screws
- Refined tine spacing for better flow distribution
