My Roborock Q7 M5+ was struggling with handling my black carpets. 

![[roborock-on-black-carpet.jpg]]
After some research, I found this was due to the cliff sensors (and there's no way in software to disable them). I only have a single floor to use it on, so I thought about removing the sensors entirely, but found out that others had success with just covering the sensors. My particular model didn't have a file published, so I made one.
