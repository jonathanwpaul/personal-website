I was running 2" electrical conduit through a wall and needed a clean mount to hold it in the 60mm hole I drilled. This cap press-fits into the hole and holds the conduit securely without any hardware.

Prints without supports. The photos show a black + white color combo from running low on black filament.

# Notes

- Fits 2" EMT or PVC conduit in a 60mm (2-3/8") hole
- A 60mm hole saw gets the right opening: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CWQ9X9DK
- Editable model on Onshape
