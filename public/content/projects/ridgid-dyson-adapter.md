I have a Dyson V7 with a bunch of useful attachments and a Ridgid shop vac — the shop vac has way more suction but I wanted to use the Dyson's tool heads with it. This adapter bridges the gap between the two hose sizes.

Designed for the V7 specifically; may work with other Dyson models but not tested.

# Notes

- Single-print, no hardware needed
- Editable CAD model available on Onshape for other vacuum hose sizes
