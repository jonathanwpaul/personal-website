A simple counter-top holder that keeps a hand towel at the kitchen sink within easy reach. It sits flat on the counter and holds the towel upright.

Features a channel for running a string through the towel to keep it from sliding off, though plastic friction is usually enough on its own.

# Notes

- Fusion 360 parametric file included for adjusting dimensions to fit your counter
- Single-piece print, no hardware needed
