I wanted a way to keep a kitchen towel handy near the stove without taking up drawer or counter space. This hook latches onto cabinet molding and lets you stuff a folded towel through it for easy access.

Inspired by bathroom towel hooks, though after living with it I think I prefer a simpler standard hook — a remixed version may come later.
